About Me

Hi, I'm currently a third-year undergrad at the University of Michigan, pursuing a double degree in Economics and Information Studies with a concentration in Information Analysis. Here are a few facts about myself...

I was born and raised in Hong Kong! I miss my hometown all the time, especially the food and the cityscape views, but definitely not the summer heat...

Last summer, I had the opportunity to intern at PwC's US tax department. I'm looking to apply my quantitative and analytical skills in the financial services industry in the upcoming summer again.

When I'm not doing school or work, I like exploring the city and taking photos of people and cityscapes. Check out my photography page!

I'm also a huge basketball fan! My favorite team is the Houston Rockets, although they're not doing too hot right now.
